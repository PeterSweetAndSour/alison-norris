Wealth In Hand

React.js, JSX, MongoDB, Mongoose, Node.js, Express.js, Token Authentication
Role: Front- and Back-End Developer
Live Site Github

Wealth In Hand is a full stack MERN application that empowers users to take control of their debt by showing them how expensive it truly is to carry debt year over year.

Personal finance is a passion of mine; I've spent many years working as a Certified Financial Planner to help clients acheive their financial goals. This tool helps to scale a subset of the advice a human would give working with clients.
